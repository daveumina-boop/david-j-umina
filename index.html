My Story

I started my delivery business in May of 1996 — six months before graduating from John Carroll University with a degree in marketing.

That same year, I got married and committed to building something that would last.

Nearly 30 years later, we've completed close to 1 million deliveries. I've seen the entire evolution of this industry — from phone orders to third-party apps.

And I’ve watched restaurants lose control of their customers in the process.

What I'm Building

Video creates demand. Restaurants capture the order directly.

No commissions. No middleman owning the customer. Just discovery → order → relationship.
