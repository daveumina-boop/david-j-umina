30 Years in Restaurant Delivery. Building What Comes Next.

My Story

I started my delivery business in 1996 — before I even graduated from John Carroll University. That same year, I got married and committed to building something that would last.

Nearly 30 years later, we've completed close to 1 million deliveries. I've seen the entire evolution of this industry — from phone orders to third-party apps.

And I’ve watched restaurants lose control of their customers in the process.

What I'm Building

DeliverMeFood is a new model:

Video creates demand. Restaurants capture the order directly.

No commissions. No middleman owning the customer. Just discovery → order → relationship.

Why This Matters

Restaurants shouldn’t have to rent their customers.

This industry is ready for a shift — and we’re building it now.
